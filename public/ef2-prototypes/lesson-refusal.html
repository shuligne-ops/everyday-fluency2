Saying no — and staying warm

No lead-in, no phrases to copy yet. Just you, first.

Marie texts you “We finally locked in Saturday for my birthday dinner — you're coming, right? Been ages.”

You genuinely can't make it — you're away that weekend. But Marie's a close friend and this matters to her. You don't want it to land as “I'd rather not.”

What do you text back? Write it the way you'd actually send it.
